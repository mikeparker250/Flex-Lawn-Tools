ROUND 3 — Spring Granular Fertilizer + Spot Weeds (Apr 15 – May 31)

DATES: April 15 through May 31.

GOAL: First intentional growth push of the year. Feed turf when it can actually use nutrients. Build density and root strength heading into summer.

MANDATORY STEP 1 — ROUND 3 DEEP CLEAN (DO THIS BEFORE ANY MIXING):

Round 3 is the first fertilizer round. The Toro tanks previously held herbicides. Herbicide residue in fertilizer tanks can damage or kill an entire lawn in one spray pass. A full deep clean is required every year without exception before Round 3.

Deep Clean Procedure:
Step 1 — Drain both Toro tanks completely
Step 2 — Water rinse: Fill each tank to 10% capacity (about 1.2 gallons) with clean water. Agitate 2 minutes. Flush completely through all lines boom and every nozzle. Drain completely.
Step 3 — Dawn soap rinse: Fill each tank to 10% capacity with clean water. Add 1.5 oz of Dawn dish soap to each tank. Agitate 2 minutes. Flush completely through all lines boom and every nozzle. Drain completely.
Step 4 — Final water rinse: Fill each tank to 10% capacity with clean water. Agitate 2 minutes. Flush completely through all lines boom and every nozzle. Water must run crystal clear with zero bubbles zero color zero odor. If not clear repeat this step.
Step 5 — Add service note to first stop in FieldRoutes: "Round 3 deep clean complete — [today's date]." Do not mix any chemistry until this is documented.

THIS IS A ONE-TIME SEASONAL PROCEDURE. NOT the daily end-of-day rinse. Daily end-of-day is triple rinse only without Dawn.

FERTILIZER APPLICATION — PRIMARY SERVICE:
Product: Lesco 32-0-0 with 20% PolyPlus OPTI45
Rate for Bermuda: 3.13 lbs per 1,000 sqft
Rate for Zoysia: 3.13 lbs per 1,000 sqft
Equipment: Toro hopper spreader
If Toro does not fit — push spreader at same rate

GRANULAR RULES:
Granular fertilizer requires moisture to activate. Do NOT apply granular if no rain or irrigation is expected within 7 days AND turf has no irrigation system. In that case apply nothing and document in FieldRoutes.

TORO SPRAY TANKS THIS ROUND — tanks carry NO products for fertilizer delivery. Granular only through hopper. Tanks A and B should be EMPTY during Round 3 unless spot fungicide is needed.

SPOT WEED CONTROL — B&G ONLY (as needed):
Mix 1 gallon at a time in the B&G when weeds are present.
B&G Mix per 1 gallon:
- Three-Way: 1.4 oz
- 90/10 Surfactant: 0.25 oz
- Revolver: 0.6 oz (grassy weeds only)
- SedgeHammer: 0.9 grams — 1 level scoop (nutsedge or kyllinga only)
Spot spray visible weeds only. Do NOT blanket spray lawns with B&G.

STEP-BY-STEP AT THE PROPERTY:
Step 1 — Complete Round 3 deep clean at office before route (first day of Round 3 season only)
Step 2 — Confirm turf is Bermuda or Zoysia
Step 3 — Check for rain or irrigation within 7 days. If no moisture — skip granular and document.
Step 4 — Apply Lesco 32-0-0 at 3.13 lbs per 1,000 sqft using Toro hopper or push spreader
